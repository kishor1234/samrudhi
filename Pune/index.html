Welcome To Chatrabhuj Narsee School

From Knowledge To Life

Chatrabhuj Narsee School at Amanora Park Town is being built on a sprawling 6.5 acre campus with state of the art infrastructure and optimal use of technology in the classrooms. With a large playground that includes professional running track and turf for multisport facilities, a metre swimming pool, a high tech indoor multi sports hall and much more.

The Nursery to 12th Grade School will offer an International Curriculum with Cambridge from Pre-primary to Grde10 followed by the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme for Grade 11 and 12.

The school will begin its operations of admissions and administration in September 2021 and commerce with teaching from the academic year starting July 2022.
